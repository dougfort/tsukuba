# Kenji — 健二

## Basic Information

- **Age:** 11
- **Name (Kanji):** 健二 or 賢二
- **Role:** Leader of the trio, older brother to Taro
- **Family:** Mori family

## Physical Description

- **Height:** 4'6" (137 cm)
- **Weight:** 73 lbs (33 kg)
- **Build:** Slim and wiry, tireless energy
- **Hair:** Straight and black, practical bowl cut maintained by his mother with kitchen scissors, always slightly uneven at the back
- **Eyes:** Dark and wide-set, giving him a look of perpetual alert curiosity
- **Distinguishing Features:** Perpetually scraped knees in various stages of healing, hands that are never quite clean in summer, his father's jaw — slightly too strong for his age
- **Expression:** Mouth goes slightly open when thinking hard; entire body turns when excited, not just his head

## Clothing

**School:** Standard uniform, correctly assembled in the morning and slowly dismantled through the day. Shirt untucked on one side by afternoon, socks surrendered.

**Casual:** Simple cotton shorts in navy or gray, plain short-sleeved shirt, thin canvas sneakers. Practical and slightly worn. Owns a wristwatch — given by his father, slightly too adult for him, but deeply prized.

## Personality

- Bold and action-oriented — the one who goes first
- Intensely curious, fully inhabiting childhood with no sense of its ending
- Natural leader by temperament rather than age or maturity
- Goes quiet when thinking, which Taro misreads as moodiness
- Runs everywhere rather than walks

## Relationships

- **Taro:** Younger brother (age 9). Responsible for him in a way he didn't choose and sometimes resents. Loves him without being able to say so.
- **Shin:** Best friend (age 10). Connected by shared curiosity. Relies on Shin's knowledge without always admitting it.
- **Hiroshi:** Father. Kindred spirits. They communicate mostly through shared silence and occasional intense conversation.
- **Akiko:** Mother. She watches him with quiet pride and careful attention.

## Family

- Son of Mori Hiroshi (earth sciences researcher) and Mori Akiko
- Father's colleague Nakamura Takashi is Shin's father — the families knew each other before Tsukuba
- Mother cuts his hair with kitchen scissors

## Nushi Connection

- First of the boys to physically enter the Slope Node
- His resonance with Nushi's cognitive activity is characterized by bold forward motion — he moves toward the presence where others might retreat
- His developing abilities will likely manifest as heightened physical intuition — knowing where to go, what to do, which way to move

## Notes

- Finds Taro's English-words t-shirt embarrassing
- At peak childhood — fully present in it, not yet sensing its end
- Accepts the name Mittsu no hoshi with a single nod toward the mountain

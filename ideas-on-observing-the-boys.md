# Observing the Boys

## Outline

* Nushi has already been paying diffuse attention to the Tsukuba research community.
* The boys begin to appear in the drainage channel
* The boys draw Nushi's attention by their presence in the Slope Node
* Nushi initiates structures to observe the boys the Tsukuba research community.
  * This is not done by laying wires or anything physical,
  * Circulating patterns for recognizing the boys
  * Distributing information requests
  * Monitoring information flow and adjusting patterns
  * There is not one central node or cluster to watch the boys, but shifting patterns of interest in the overall network
  * Some clusters of nodes "learn" the behavior of the boys.

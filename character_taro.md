# Taro — 太郎

## Basic Information

- **Age:** 9
- **Name (Kanji):** 太郎
- **Role:** Younger brother to Kenji, chaotic element of the trio
- **Family:** Mori family

## Physical Description

- **Height:** 4'2" (127 cm)
- **Weight:** 62 lbs (28 kg)
- **Build:** Small for his age, still carrying some softness of early childhood
- **Hair:** Straight and black, never lies flat regardless of his mother's morning efforts
- **Eyes:** Very bright and dark, moving constantly — missing nothing while appearing to pay attention to nothing
- **Distinguishing Features:** Gap-toothed smile that goes slightly higher on the left, one ear fractionally higher than the other, small scar through his left eyebrow from an incident the family doesn't discuss without sighing. Everything about him is slightly asymmetrical in an endearing way.
- **Expression:** Round-cheeked and animated, assembled quickly where Kenji was assembled carefully

## Clothing

**School:** Uniform that looks like it was put on during an earthquake regardless of how it started the day. Mother has given up on the socks. Randoseru slightly too large for him, giving the impression of a backpack wearing a small boy.

**Casual:** Whatever he grabbed. A treasured t-shirt with English words nobody in the family can fully translate. Shorts that may or may not match. Canvas sneakers with one lace perpetually coming undone. A favorite red baseball cap, absolutely filthy, present in roughly half his waking hours.

## Personality

- Loud, curious, completely lacking his brother's filter between thought and speech
- Asks the embarrassingly obvious question nobody else thought to ask
- Stubbornness that appears to be a flaw until it suddenly becomes exactly what's needed
- Absorbs far more than anyone around him realizes
- Small enough that he can't quite keep up physically, stubborn enough to absolutely refuse to accept that fact

## Relationships

- **Kenji:** Older brother (age 11). Idolizes him completely while driving him crazy with equal completeness. Deeply aware of the four inch height difference and considers it a personal injustice.
- **Shin:** Tolerated by Shin with affectionate exasperation, which Taro reciprocates with cheerful indifference.
- **Hiroshi:** Father. Loves him with a kind of delighted helplessness.
- **Akiko:** Mother. A relationship of loving exhaustion on her side, complete confidence on his.

## Family

- Younger son of Mori Hiroshi (earth sciences researcher) and Mori Akiko
- Traditional name may reflect the influence of a more conservative grandparent
- Same household as Kenji — overhears scientific conversations slightly above his head and absorbs more than anyone suspects

## Nushi Connection

- Nushi finds Taro's apparent randomness mathematically interesting — like a fractal that reveals deeper order the longer you look
- Takes personal credit for choosing such a good territory when Nushi makes the drainage channel more hospitable
- His developing abilities may be the most surprising — what looks like chaos may be a form of pattern recognition operating below conscious awareness

## Notes

- The scar through his left eyebrow has a story. The family sighs when it comes up.
- His English words t-shirt is a prized possession. Shin knows exactly what it says and whether the grammar is correct.
- Immediately and loudly approves of the name Mittsu no hoshi
- Loves the name unreservedly and uses it at every opportunity, including inappropriate ones
